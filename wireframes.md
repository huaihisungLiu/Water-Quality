# Wireframes

**Status: ⚪ Upcoming — not started yet**

## What this step is

Wireframes are the first look at what Poop Patrol actually *is* to a kid using it — simple
sketches of the screens and how you move between them, before any real design or code.
They turn the positioning statement into something you can point at and react to.

## The results so far

No wireframes have been created yet. This step comes after the positioning statement is
grounded in research and interviews, so there's nothing to show here just yet.

Once wireframes exist, this is where they'll live — and they'll also be featured on the
**[Home page]({{ '/' | relative_url }})**, since seeing the experience is the clearest way
to explain what the project is building.
